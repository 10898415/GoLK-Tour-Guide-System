Trincomalee is a beautiful coastal town on the northeast coast of Sri Lanka, just 16 meters above sea level. It is famous for its stunning beaches like Nilaveli and Uppuveli, where visitors can relax, swim, and enjoy the warm sunshine.\n\nThe town is also home to the Koneswaram Temple, a sacred Hindu site on a cliff with amazing ocean views. Trincomalee is a great place for whale watching, snorkeling, and diving, especially near Pigeon Island. With its peaceful beaches, clear waters, and cultural sites, Trincomalee is perfect for anyone looking for a relaxing and enjoyable trip. 57000
