Sigiriya Rock A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this ancient rock fortress features the ruins of a palace atop a 200-meter-high rock, surrounded by extensive gardens, moats, and frescoes.
Pidurangala Rock A moderately challenging hike leading to panoramic views, including a clear view of Sigiriya Rock.
Minneriya National Park Known for "The Gathering," where large herds of elephants congregate, especially during the dry season.
Hiriwadunna Village Experience traditional Sri Lankan village life, including bullock cart rides, boat trips on the lake, and authentic local meals.
Elephant Rides and Safaris Several operators offer elephant-back rides and safaris in the Sigiriya area, providing a unique perspective of the landscape.
