Badulla is a scenic hill town in Sri Lanka, located at 680 meters above sea level. Surrounded by lush tea plantations, waterfalls, and green hills, it offers a perfect mix of natural beauty and cultural heritage. The town is home to the Muthiyangana Temple, one of the most sacred Buddhist sites in Sri Lanka, believed to have been visited by the Buddha himself.\n\nVisitors can explore the breathtaking Dunhinda Falls, a cascading waterfall surrounded by tropical forest, or take a leisurely walk through the tea estates nearby. The charming train journey to Badulla, passing through tunnels and over bridges, is an unforgettable experience. With its peaceful atmosphere and stunning surroundings, Badulla is an excellent destination for those seeking tranquility and nature. 66000 Badulla Dunhinda Falls A stunning 64-meter waterfall renowned for its misty spray, creating a 'smoky' appearance. A 1.5 km trek through lush terrain leads to the viewing platform, making it a favorite among nature lovers and hikers.
